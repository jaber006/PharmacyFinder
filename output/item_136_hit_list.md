### FTE Verification is Critical
Many centres show estimated FTE based on headcount × 0.8 ratio. The actual FTE count needs manual verification via:
- AHPRA register search for each practitioner
- Direct inquiry to centre management
- Medicare provider number counts

### Competition Density vs Distance
Don't confuse "number of pharmacies in 5km" with competition for your specific location. An Item 136 pharmacy INSIDE a medical centre captures a captive patient base. The key metric is:
1. Distance to nearest pharmacy (must be >300m)
2. Whether that nearest pharmacy is a chain or independent
3. Whether there's physical space inside the centre

### Regional vs Metro
- **Metro (Melbourne/Perth/Brisbane/Adelaide/Sydney):** Higher population but higher competition. Harder to get commercial terms.
- **Regional (Burnie, Gladstone, Geraldton, Port Augusta, Nowra):** Lower competition, easier approval, but lower volume. Regional incentives may apply (MMM classification).
