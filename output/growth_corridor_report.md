## Methodology

1. **Population data:** ABS Census 2021 baseline + ERP estimates + known growth rates
   from state planning authorities (VPA, NSW DPE, QLD DSDILGP, WAPC, PlanSA).
2. **Pharmacy gap:** Counted pharmacies within 5km and 10km of each SA2 centroid.
   Ratios calculated against national average of ~4,000 people per pharmacy.
3. **Retail pipeline:** Curated database of planned town centres from Stockland,
   Lendlease, Frasers, Oreana, and other major developers + PlanningAlerts DA data.
4. **Scoring:** Weighted composite score with classification thresholds.

**Limitations:**
- Population projections use growth rates from planning documents, not actuarial forecasts
- Pharmacy counts are point-in-time from FindAPharmacy data
- Retail pipeline data relies on public announcements — some projects may be
  unannounced or have changed timelines
- SA2 centroids may not represent the actual development location within the SA2
