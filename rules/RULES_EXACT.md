# Pharmacy Location Rules — Exact Requirements (Jan 2024 v1.9)
Source: Applicant's Handbook, health.gov.au

## Item 130: New pharmacy (at least 1.5 km)
**(a)** Proposed premises ≥ 1.5km **straight line** from nearest approved premises
**(b)** Within 500m straight line of proposed premises, EITHER:
  - (i) ≥ 1 FTE prescribing medical practitioner AND supermarket ≥ 1,000m² GLA, OR
  - (ii) Supermarket ≥ 2,500m² GLA

## Item 131: New pharmacy (at least 10 km)
Proposed premises ≥ 10km by **shortest lawful access route** from nearest approved premises.
- That's it. Single requirement (plus general requirements).
- **Restriction:** Cannot ever relocate from the town where originally granted.

## Item 132: New additional pharmacy (at least 10 km)
**(a)** Proposed premises:
  - (i) In the **same town** (same name + same postcode) as an approved premises
  - (ii) ≥ 200m **straight line** from the **nearest** approved premises
  - (iii) ≥ 10km by **shortest lawful access route** from **ALL other** approved premises (excluding the one in (ii))
**(b)** In the same town as proposed premises:
  - (i) ≥ 4 FTE prescribing medical practitioners practising
  - (ii) 1-2 supermarkets with **combined** GLA ≥ 2,500m²
- **Restriction:** Cannot ever relocate from the town where originally granted.

### Key points for Item 132:
- The "10km" in (a)(iii) is from ALL other pharmacies except the nearest one, by ROAD not straight line
- The 4 FTE GPs must be in the SAME TOWN (same name + postcode)
- The supermarkets must also be in the SAME TOWN
- "Full-time" = 38 hours/week for a medical practitioner
- GLA excludes loading docks and car parks
- GLA includes trading floor + minimal back of house (≤25-30%)

## Item 133: New pharmacy in small shopping centre
**(a)** Proposed premises in a **small shopping centre**:
  - Single management
  - GLA ≥ 5,000m²
  - Contains supermarket ≥ 2,500m² GLA
  - ≥ 15 other commercial establishments
  - Customer parking
**(b)** ≥ 500m straight line from nearest approved premises (excluding those in large shopping centre or private hospital)
**(c)** No approved premises in the small shopping centre

## Item 134: New pharmacy in large shopping centre (no existing pharmacy)
**(a)** Proposed premises in a **large shopping centre**:
  - Single management
  - GLA ≥ 5,000m²
  - Contains supermarket ≥ 2,500m² GLA
  - ≥ 50 other commercial establishments
  - Customer parking
**(b)** No approved premises in the large shopping centre

## Item 134A: New additional pharmacy in large shopping centre (with existing pharmacy)
**(a)** Proposed premises in a **large shopping centre** (same definition as 134)
**(b)** Tenant count determines max pharmacies:
  - 100-199 commercial establishments → max 1 existing pharmacy (allows 2nd)
  - ≥ 200 commercial establishments → max 2 existing pharmacies (allows 3rd)
**(c)** No pharmacy has relocated OUT of the centre in last 12 months

## Item 135: New pharmacy in large private hospital
**(a)** Proposed premises in a **large private hospital** (can admit ≥ 150 patients at any time)
**(b)** No approved premises in the hospital

## Item 136: New pharmacy in large medical centre
**(a)** Proposed premises in a **large medical centre**:
  - Single management
  - Operates ≥ 70 hours/week
  - ≥ 1 prescribing medical practitioner available for ≥ 70 hours/week
**(b)** No approved premises in the medical centre
**(c)** Distance requirements:
  - If centre is in a shopping centre/hospital: ≥ 300m straight line from any approved premises EXCEPT those in a DIFFERENT large shopping centre or hospital
  - If centre is NOT in a shopping centre/hospital: ≥ 300m straight line from nearest approved premises EXCEPT those in a large shopping centre or hospital
**(d)** During 2 months before application AND until consideration day:
  - ≥ 8 FTE PBS prescribers (38hrs/week each = 304 hrs/week total)
  - Of which ≥ 7 must be prescribing medical practitioners
  - Max 38hrs/week from non-medical PBS prescribers (dentists, optometrists, midwives, nurse practitioners)
**(e)** Applicant will make reasonable attempts to match medical centre operating hours

### Key points for Item 136:
- "Full-time" = 38 hours/week. Even if one GP works 57hrs, that's only 1.5 FTE
- Telehealth only counts if GP is PHYSICALLY at the centre
- Time at other centres, hospitals, nursing homes, admin does NOT count
- The 8 FTE threshold is STRICT — must be maintained for 2+ months
- 300m distance is straight line, not road

## Glossary — Critical Definitions
- **Same town:** Same town name AND same postcode
- **Full-time:** 38 hours/week
- **GLA (supermarket):** Total floor area excluding loading docks and car parks. Includes trading floor + back of house (≤25-30%). Does NOT include shipping containers or separate storage tenancies.
- **GLA (shopping centre):** Total floor area excluding loading docks and car parks.
- **Commercial establishment:** Shop/bar/cafe/restaurant/takeaway/service business. Excludes: offices (accountants, lawyers, etc unless shopfront), government offices, car wash/parking, library, childcare (unless for shoppers), storage, ATMs, temporary selling points. Two+ establishments by same business = 1.
- **Large shopping centre:** Single management + GLA ≥ 5,000m² + supermarket ≥ 2,500m² + ≥ 50 commercial establishments + parking
- **Small shopping centre:** Same but ≥ 15 commercial establishments
- **Large medical centre:** Single management + operates ≥ 70hrs/week + GP available ≥ 70hrs/week
- **Large private hospital:** Can admit ≥ 150 patients at any time
- **Shortest lawful access route:** Generally available route by car/walking/legal travel. From mid-point of ground-level public access door to door. Must be available to most public.
- **Straight line:** From mid-point at ground level of public access door to public access door. Use nearest doors if multiple.
